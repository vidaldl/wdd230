Join Us!

Apply for a membership using the form below. A member of the chamber will be in contact with you soon after. Join us and discover how we can serve you and how you can serve the community!

Membership Levels Benefits:

  1. NP Membership
    • For non profit organizations and there is no fee
  2. Bronze Membership
    • 15% discount on events
    • 10% discount on advertising
    • Fee of $50/month
  3. Silver Membership
    • 25% discount on events
    • 15% discount on advertising
    • Free participation in the trade fair
    • Fee of $100/month
  4. Gold Membership
    • 50% discount on events
    • 50% discount on advertising
    • Free participation in the trade fair
    • 5 free featured ads per year
    • 1 free training per quarter
    • Fee of $250/month
