Site Purpose

This website will be a portal that will bring all of the services and answers to quick questions that the user might have. It will be designed to display what the city of Saint Domingue represents and what it has available for the citizens, businesses, and organizations that belong in the city.

The Saint Domingue Chamber of Commerce website will be designed to showcase how the organization has transformed the city into a better place with various examples of past projects and outcome changes the organization has been responsible for. Showing action instead of mere words, this portal will be a hub for every person interested in knowing more about the chamber and it's services.

Last but not least, it will have the presence of many local businesses and historical sites that invite the user to experience the Saint Domingue's rich culture.
